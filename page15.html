Our Objectives

Laboratory & computational pipelines

Implement and validate relevant laboratory (initially PacBio, Hi-C and multi tissue transcriptomics) and computational pipelines to ensure efficient sequencing, assembly and annotation of eukaryotic genomes.

Technologies 

Explore the implementation of complementary technologies (e.g. Oxford Nanopore) to improve resulting genome quality and sequencing efficiency, where relevant.

Public databases

Release resulting genomes to public databases listing species in process, completed (including status updates), collaborators derived from and links to resulting data.
