Commercialization of Research

UCPH researchers had an opportunity to hear from the University of Copenhagen's Open Entrepreneurship | Innovation & External Relations department on innovation processes within an academic setting. The "dos and don'ts" of innovation are crucial for researchers to understand, especially when considering potential commercial applications of their work.

The presentation covered a range of topics, while questions like:

1. Why should I commercialise my research?

2. How can I assess the commercial potential of my research?

3. What is the commercialization process?

4. How can I extend my knowledge on innovation at UCPH?

Overall, the presentation aimed to empower researchers with the knowledge and tools they need to navigate the complex landscape of innovation and commercialization effectively. By equipping them with practical advice and resources, universities can help researchers maximize the impact of their work and contribute to societal and economic advancement.
