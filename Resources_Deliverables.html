D4.3 Analysis of host-microbiome dynamics in gut microenvironment

Salmonids have a few dominant gut bacteria species. Healthy fish coexist with Mycoplasma sp., but stress lets harmful bacteria like Aliivibrio sp. spread. After an infection, Mycoplasma sp. doesn't recover, and Aliivibrio sp. often takes over, harming fish growth.

We created a model showing how the fish immune system, Mycoplasma sp., and harmful bacteria interact. The model confirms that Mycoplasma helps prevent pathogens, but stress weakens the immune system, allowing pathogens to take over.
